Who are we ?

We are a group of people that believe in the value of home-cooked meals. Our objective is to encourage people to eat home cooked food by empowering home cooked food delivery start-ups. We are achieving our aim by using the Krazy Craving common platform to link hungry stomachs with nearby wonderful home cooked food merchants.
